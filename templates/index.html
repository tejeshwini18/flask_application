Tejeshwini (born:18 May 2000) is a System Engineer at Tata Consultancy Services(TCS),Bangalore. She is from Bemetara Chhattisgarh.


Tejeshwini has completed her 10th standard from Saraswati High School ,Gandai with 95.5% in the year 2015. Then she went to Prayas Residential School ,Raipur & completed her 12th standard with 95% in the year 2017. She has completed her graduation from National Institute of Technology(NIT),Raipur in Electronics & Telecommunications Engineering and Honoured with B.Tech Hon. Degree in the year 2021.


Tejeshwini is a goal-oriented and a highly creative Software engineer looking for a challenging role to enhance the company’s growth as well as expand her knowledge to enhance her performance.


Tejeshwini is fond of Coding, Watching thriller movies ,Gardening, travelling, Reading Books ,Teaching etc.


Tejeshwini has done 14 days vocational training from “CSPDCL(Chhattisgarh State Power Distribution Company Limited) Raipur” and “Bhilai Steel Plant,Bhilai” on “Power Line Carrier Communication” and “Working Overview Of BSP” respectively.
